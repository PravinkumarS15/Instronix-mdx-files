<h4>Date - 25/April/2024</h4>
<h4>Time - 2.00 pm</h4>
<h4>Entry Fee - 200 INR per team</h4>
<h4>Venue - EIE Classroom, EI210</h4>
<h3>Rules :</h3>

1. Each team consists of a maximum of 4 members.
2. Bring your college Id card and screenshot of your payment.

First Round:

- Each team receives a set of question papers with 25 MCQs related to cricket.
- 30 minutes given for answering.
- Mobile phones are strictly prohibited.
- Top 8 teams will be shortlisted for the 2nd round.

Second Round: Auction Round

- All eight teams receive a specific amount of money.
- Teams can only buy players with the allotted money.
- Team Composition
  - 4 Foreign Players
  - 7 Indian Players
  - 3-5 Batsmen
  - 1-3 All-Rounders
  - 4-5 Bowlers (Minimum 2 Spinners, Minimum 2 Fast Bowlers)
  - 1 Wicket Keeper
  - 1 Coach

Determining the Winning Team:

- Winning team will be declared based on the above criteria.
- If multiple teams satisfy the above criteria then the winning team will be declared on player's T20 ICC rating.
