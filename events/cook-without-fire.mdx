<h3>Rules :</h3>

- No pre-cooked items should be bought.
- Each team must have two members.
- Participants are supposed to display a chart having the name, ingredients, and calorie count of the dish prepared.
- All items required for cooking should be bought by the participants.
- Dishes should be purely vegetarian.
- Participants should not bring any cut, chopped raw materials. It must be prepared on the spot within the allotted time.
- Participants can bring non-electric equipment like a mixer, chopper, juicer.
- Evaluation will be based on taste, nutritional level of the dish, presentation.
