<h3>Rules :</h3>

- This is an Individual Contest.
- This is a coding contest based on algorithms, data structures, and problem solving.
- Competitors will be tasked with a series of algorithmic challenges of varying difficulty while working under the pressure of a strict time limit to produce their solutions in Java, Python, C++, or C.
- Allowed Programming Languages: Java, Python, C++, C#, C.
All participants must bring their own laptops.
Three Rounds are conducted, each being an elimination round.
  - 1st Round consists of basic problems.
  - 2nd Round consists of an Advanced coding Problem.
  - 3rd Round consists of an Advanced coding Problem with High complexity.
- Participants who solve the problem within the time limit will proceed to the next round.
- In case of a tie, time complexity and space complexity will be analyzed for the result.
- Organizer results will be final.
- If any team is caught cheating, that team will be disqualified.
