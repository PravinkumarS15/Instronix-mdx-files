<h4>Date - 25/April/2024</h4>
<h4>Time - 3.00 pm</h4>
<h4>Entry Fee - 150 INR per team</h4>
<h4>Venue - Auditorium Multipurpose hall</h4>


<h3>Rules</h3>
- Three memebers in a Team.
- Bring your college Id card and screenshot of your payment.

<h4>Round 1: Act It Out</h4>

- One person enacts(act), and the team members must guess.
- Time duration is one minute.
- Sounds, lettering, or splits per word are not allowed. 
- Pointing to an object or person or showing something is not allowed.

<h4>Round 2: Pictionary Round</h4>

- The participant must draw the given word and convey the meaning to his/her team members. 
- Time duration is 1 minute.
- No action is allowed. No sounds are allowed.

<h4>Round 3: Track Me</h4>

- One member of the team will be given a set of words and shall begin a story. 
- On the prompt of the member, the next member will take over, and then the next to get a complete story. 
- Each member will get the time of 30 seconds.

*(This is a group event. If anyone wants to join as a team, make sure to fill the form together as a team. Else you can also form a team on spot and participate.)*
