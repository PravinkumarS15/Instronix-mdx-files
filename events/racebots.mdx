<h4>Date - 24/April/2024</h4>
<h4>Time - 3.00 pm</h4>
<h4>Entry Fee - 150 INR per team</h4>
<h4>Venue - EIE department corridor</h4>
<h3>Rules :</h3>

- A team consists of maximum of 3 members.
- The robot must be autonomous/automatic.
- In competition, ready-made robots are not allowed.
- Participants must arrange their batteries.
- Power supply must be on board.
- Electrical voltage of the robot should not exceed 12 Volt (DC) at any time during the competition.
- All building materials are allowed in construction until the robot satisfies all the above conditions.
- Ready-made robots aren't allowed to compete in the event.
- The above event is not a line-following bot; it's a bot which should move forward, backward, turn right, and left.
- All must bring your college ID card and screenshot of your payment.
