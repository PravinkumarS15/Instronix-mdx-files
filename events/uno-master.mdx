<h3>Rules :</h3>

- Each game can be played by 5 to 10 players. Each player gets 7 cards.
- The objective of each round of Uno is to be the first player to get rid of all their cards.
- First player begins, and each player has to put a single card from their hand onto the discard pile, but only if it has a matching colour, number or symbol.
- If a card canâ€™t be played, that player must pick up a new card from the draw pile and then skip the rest of their go.
- Wildcard color card can be played when the player needs to change the color.
- Wildcard +4 is only played when the player doesn't have matching color with discard pile.
- The player must recite uno when they play the last before card. If not recited and found by the opponents then the player have to draw two cards.
- The player that empties all the cards they have, wins.
